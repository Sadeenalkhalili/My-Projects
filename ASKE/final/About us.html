ASKE was founded in 2012 by a group of information technology experts .It's an e-commerce website for selling items . part of the outcome is going to be donated for charities to provide a small help for our world. We were inspired by the idea of establishing the site to do business for us and to help the community,Our Business is built on the basis of "Get and Give" logo.

The story of the founding of the website began when a disease pandemic spread 10 years ago that affected the entire world, so all countries took measures to reduce workers and stop them from working to prevent the spread of the disease, and we were among those who were suspended from their work during that period.so we came up with the idea of creating a website from which we can work and provide our needs from it and to make our own buisness.our site sell multiple kinds of objects u want and all are made in switzerland.

ASKE has donated more than 5 million relief items to support people impacted by over 95 natural disasters around the world....We got a lot of certificates over the years for our donations and our support for alot of entities.
